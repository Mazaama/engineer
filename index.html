About Us

Tejas Energy and Infra Resources is a dynamic and rapidly growing company dedicated to delivering world-class solutions in the fields of Oil & Gas, Infrastructure, Engineering, and Global Manpower Services. With a strong commitment to quality, safety, and innovation, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for clients across multiple industries and geographies.

Our core strength lies in our ability to provide end-to-end solutions — from highly skilled manpower deployment to complete project execution in complex industrial environments. We specialize in supporting large-scale projects such as refineries, industrial plants, road construction, heavy lifting operations, and building engineering.

Driven by a team of experienced professionals and industry experts, we ensure that every project is executed with precision, efficiency, and adherence to international standards. Our workforce is trained to meet the demanding requirements of global markets, making us a reliable partner for both domestic and international clients.

At Tejas Energy and Infra Resources, we believe in building long-term relationships based on trust, transparency, and performance excellence. Our commitment to delivering value goes beyond project completion — we aim to contribute to the sustainable growth and development of the industries we serve.

Under the visionary leadership of our Managing Director, Pinki, the company continues to expand its footprint and capabilities, striving to become a leading name in the global energy and infrastructure sector.

We are not just a service provider — we are your strategic partner in progress, empowering businesses with the right talent, technology, and solutions.

Our Mission: To deliver high-quality engineering and manpower solutions that exceed client expectations while maintaining the highest standards of safety and integrity.

Our Vision: To be recognized as a globally trusted leader in energy, infrastructure, and industrial services.

Our Values: Integrity • Excellence • Innovation • Commitment • Safety
